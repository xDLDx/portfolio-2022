I'm Dong Liu (He/him/his). Currently, I'm studying UX design at University of Michigan’s School of Information as an exchange student from Uppsala University (Sweden) where I study Game Design.

I'm passionate about creating pleasurable digital experiences to make life easier. Therefore, I started my journey to experience different cultures and expand my understanding of users and the world. In the past 5 years, I lived in 4 countries on 3 continents. I believe the global vision I gained from both eastern and western culture can enrich my creativity to solve global issues with other aspiring individuals.

Looking forward to applying my knowledge and skills to UX Design in related fields.

In Summer 2021, I practiced my skills with real-world problems as a product management intern at OPPO + OnePlus Group (a Chinese smartphone company). During the internship, I collaborated with co-workers all over the world, which helped me to develop cross-cultural communication skills. Meanwhile I also gained PRD writing, usability testing and prototyping skills through working across the entire product lifecycle with Google to design realmeUI for pad (an Android custom ROM). At the same time, I created the wireframes of the new feature “Scene” for realme Link 2.0 which had about 400,000 weekly active users, and ColorOS 12 ’s control widget for IoT devices which was used by 500 Million global users monthly.
