My Design Philosophy: First Principle Design Thinking:“A problem well-stated is half-solved"--Charles Kettering. As a UX designer, I spend most of my time to understand the users and their needs. I keep asking myself "why?" again and again with multiple user research methods since I need to understand the fundamental principles and reasons behind those needs. Therefore, I can breakdown all the problems into these most basic truths, and use them to create new solutions outside the box.

As an international student at Uppsala University, I learned from Swedish design philosophy : Logam, which means “Just the right amount”. It inspires me to practice reasonable designs to create continuous and delightful digital experiences.
